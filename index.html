NewRoots Refugee Artists in the Netherlands

In a world marked by conflict, economic instability, and forced migration, millions are compelled to rebuild their lives from nothing. Among them are artists and creative professionals — individuals who carry not only their personal histories but also the cultural heritage of their homelands.

“New Roots” is an initiative that supports refugee artists in the Netherlands by creating opportunities for integration, visibility, and professional development through a multidisciplinary exhibition and accompanying public program.

We invite artists, photographers, dancers, performers, musicians, and representatives of other creative professions. "New Roots" is an initiative that supports refugee artists in the Netherlands.

Please send files of your work with details to newrootsexhibition@gmail.com
Belonging

“New Roots” is more than an exhibition - it is a bridge between worlds, a space for dialogue, creativity, and human connection. By supporting this initiative, the foundation will help ensure that refugee artists not only find safety but also reclaim their voices and contribute to the shared cultural future of their new home.

Impact
  • Empower refugees to regain professional identity and confidence.
  • Create meaningful connections between newcomers and the Dutch creative community.
  • Enrich the cultural landscape of the Netherlands by showcasing diverse artistic voices and stories.
Objectives The project aims to:
  • Provide a creative platform for refugee artists, musiciansand performers to present their work to the Dutch public.
  • Foster cultural exchange and understanding between refugees and local communities.
  • Facilitate integration into the Dutch cultural sector through mentorship, networking, and professional guidance
Program Components The initiative will take place in collaboration with COA (Centraal Orgaan opvang asielzoekers) and will include:
  • Art Exhibition featuring works by refugee artists.
  • Music - exhibition with headphones for listening to music: DJ sets, instrumental music, and more
  • Music and Theatre Performances highlighting diverse cultural expressions.
  • Experience Exchange Sessions with artists who have successfully integrated in past years.
  • Lectures and Workshops led by curators, producers, and representatives of cultural foundations on professional development in the Netherlands.
  • Public Talk and Q&A to encourage dialogue between participants and audiences.
